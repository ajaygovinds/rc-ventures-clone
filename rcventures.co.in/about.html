About Us

About Us

We, RC Ventures are one of the leading distributors, and suppliers of various varieties of plastic crates, industrial crates, dairy crates, bottle crates, fishery crates, fabricated crates, fruit crates, vegetable crates roto giant crates, waste bins, injection moulded plastic pallets, waste trolleys, roto waste bin, chill pads and roto moulded pallets. These are manufactured using quality virgin plastic and stainless steel to ensure high durability and light weight for better handling. The promoters of RC Ventures have more than 2 decades of experience in this field, and also employ very experienced and professional personnel for all customer requirements.

These are widely appreciated because of the following features

  • Immense strength
  • Attractive designs
  • Fine finish
  • Durable

Further, we also customize our Plastic Crates, Pallets and Waste Bins as per the specifications detailed by our clients.

Quality Assurance

We follow strict quality control policies to give our customers the quality products. Our adherence to ethical business practices and qualitative approach has given us an edge over the other leading organizations. Right from the initial stage of procurement of raw material till the final delivery of the consignment, we undertake stringent quality control measures.

Tour quality analysts stringently check our range of products on well-defined parameters such as:

  • Load capacity to ensure that the crates, pallets and waste bins can be used effectively to transport and store heavy items
  • Strength of our products is of vital importance, so we conduct various tests to ensure that they can withstand heavy weight
  • We ensure that our plastic products are made corrosion resistant and resistant to acid and alkali solutions

With the help of these quality control parameters, we have maintained a strong position in the industry. Our advanced methodology and innovative techniques enable us to manufacture our range of products in compliance with international norms. Further, we also update our testing machines and tools on regular bases so that we can provide effective plastic crates, pallets and waste bins to our valued clients.

Warehousing Packaging

Our warehousing unit has high storage capacity to stock our range of Plastic Crates, Pallets and Waste Bins in bulk quantity. It has been internally divided into several sections to facilitate an easy accommodation, access and retrieval of our Plastic Crates, Pallets and Waste Bins.

Tour quality analysts stringently check our range of products on well-defined parameters such as

  • Spacious
  • Highly organized
  • Fire safety arrangements
  • Well connected with various modes of transport

Besides, we also pay special heed in effective packaging of our range of products so that they do not get damaged in transit. We make use of quality packing material to ensure compliance with environment friendly standards enforced worldwide. In this way we are able to offer our clients with tamper free Plastic Crates, Pallets and Waste Bins.
