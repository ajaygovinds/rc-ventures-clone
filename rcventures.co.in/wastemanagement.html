Waste Management

Waste Management Division

With the Directives of Municipal Solid Waste Handling Management Rules, 2000; we offer optimum waste management and disposal system. These systems are useful in storage, collection and safe disposal of waste at individual and community level. We are working with OTTO Waste management Systems, Germany for supply of high tech plastic waste bins to Municipalities, Super markets, Multiplexes, Housing colonies etc. These bins are compatible with Strict International EN/DIN norms for HDPE waste containers and various lifting devices built as per the EN/DIN norms. Apart from being durable and eco- friendly, these bins are engineered to resist high impact.

We also offer free stand litter bins under the Brand name of PENTA, manufactured from the optimum quality raw materials, our waste bin is highly durable. Our garbage waste bin is light in weight, thus easily movable. All of the bins are provided with different type of lids that can be opened and easily clean the bins.
